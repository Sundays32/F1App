2024 Season

The 2024 Formula 1 season was a thrilling spectacle of speed, strategy, and standout performances. Max Verstappen of Red Bull Racing dominated the championship, securing his position as the driver to beat with an impressive tally of 277 points, 7 wins, and 9 podium finishes. McLaren's Lando Norris proved a formidable challenger, finishing second with 199 points, although his lone win was not enough to surpass Verstappen. Charles Leclerc of Ferrari rounded out the top three, showcasing consistent performance with 177 points and 1 win. Despite a strong season from the top contenders, the season also highlighted the resurgence of Mercedes, with Lewis Hamilton and George Russell delivering competitive results. Aston Martin and Ferrari demonstrated their potential, though neither could match the relentless pace of Red Bull. Teams like Haas, Alpine, and Williams struggled throughout the season, while newcomers and underdogs made for compelling storylines. The 2024 season solidified Verstappen's reputation and set the stage for an exciting future in F1, with teams and drivers already gearing up for the next chapter in their racing careers.
