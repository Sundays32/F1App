Prominent rumours in Formula 1

Newey's future

Newey joins Aston Martin/ Mclaren?

As of mid-2024, rumors are circulating about Adrian Newey, the renowned chief technical officer at Red Bull Racing, potentially moving to another Formula 1 team. Newey, celebrated for his innovative designs and pivotal role in Red Bull's recent successes, is rumored to be in discussions with several top teams. Speculation suggests that his next destination could be one of the leading teams looking to leverage his expertise to boost their performance. Reports indicate that Mclaren and Aston Martin are among the teams interested in Newey, aiming to capitalize on his engineering prowess to gain a competitive edge. The rumors have been fueled by various sources, including reports from motorsport news outlets and insider information shared within the F1 community.

Kimi Antonelli lined up for Mercedes seat

Has Kimi signed for 2025 already?

Recent rumours have surfaced that Kimi Antonelli has already signed for Mercedes F1 team for the 2025 season alongside George Russell. The team feels they can replicate the success of Oscar Piastri and Mclaren with Kimi very soon. Rumours also talk about Mercedes being in talks to sign Max Verstappen for 2026 season, when major engine regulations are supposed to come into effect which looks to benefit Mercedes heavily.

Is Alpine/Renault engine program dead?

Has Alpine signed up to become Mercedes customer team?

Rumours in the paddock suggest that the management of Alpine F1 team are planning to shut down their engine program in favour of becoming a customer team of Mercedes engine program. They would join Mclaren and Williams as the customers, with Aston Martin moving to partner with Honda. Alpine motorsport division say that the resources tied up in the engine program will be restructured and utilised for its development program for World Endurance Championship efforts
